    Hey Members…

    We believe the best care happens when doctors work together.

    That’s why we actively collaborate with our members’ existing doctors and specialists—serving as a central point of coordination rather than a replacement. With your consent, we communicate, consult, and align with your treating physicians to ensure continuity of care, shared clinical context, and thoughtful decision-making.

    Our role is to connect the dots: integrating inputs from multiple providers, avoiding fragmented care, and making sure nothing gets lost between appointments—so your healthcare feels seamless, informed, and truly personalized.

    Hey Doctors…

    We see ourselves as collaborators, not competitors.

    Our concierge primary care model is designed to work alongside you as a clinical partner. With patient consent, we share relevant history, longitudinal insights, and follow-up data, and remain available for discussion or consultation as needed. Our goal is to support continuity of care, reduce fragmentation, and ensure alignment across treatment plans—while fully respecting your expertise and ongoing doctor–patient relationship.

    By acting as a consistent point of contact for our shared patients, we help close gaps between visits, monitor progress, and escalate concerns early—so care remains coordinated, efficient, and patient-centered.
